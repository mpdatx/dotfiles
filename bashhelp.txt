M-b  : back one word                C-a: beginning of line
M-f  : forward one word             C-e: end of line
M-del: Delete word before cursor    C-u: Delete to beginning of line
M-d  : Delete word after cursor     C-k: Delete to end of line

C-xx: toggle between start of line/current position
